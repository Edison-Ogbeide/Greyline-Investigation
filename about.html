Who We Are

Intelligence-led security & investigation

Greyline Investigations is a professional cyber intelligence, risk, and investigation firm, registered in the United Kingdom and operating globally. We serve clients across the UK, Nigeria, and the wider diaspora — including the USA, Canada, and Australia.

We combine deep OSINT capability with enterprise-grade cybersecurity services — penetration testing, threat intelligence, corporate due diligence, digital forensics, and litigation support. Every engagement is intelligence-led, legally compliant, and delivered to the highest standard.

We operate under UK law and in full compliance with the UK GDPR, the Data Protection Act 2018, and the Investigatory Powers Act 2016. For Nigerian client engagements we additionally adhere to the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 and the Cybercrimes (Prohibition, Prevention, Etc.) Act 2015. Every report is prepared to the standard required for court submission in the relevant jurisdiction.

Based in the United Kingdom, with partner investigators and cybersecurity specialists covering Nigeria (Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, and Kano). All cases are handled fully remotely — clients communicate with us on UK GMT hours, with flexible scheduling available for US and Canadian clients.
