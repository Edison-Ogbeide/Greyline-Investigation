Our Approach

Intelligence-led, legally compliant, evidence-driven

Greyline Investigations combines deep OSINT capability with enterprise-grade cybersecurity services. We serve clients across the UK, Nigeria, and the wider diaspora — including the USA, Canada, and Australia.

Every engagement starts with understanding the problem. We apply intelligence methodology tailored to your specific situation — not templates. All work is conducted under UK law, UK GDPR, the Data Protection Act 2018, and applicable international data protection standards.

We operate fully remotely. Clients communicate with us on UK GMT hours, with flexible scheduling for US and Canadian time zones. Partner investigators cover Nigeria (Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, and Kano).
