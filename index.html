Our Standards

What we do — and what we don't

We always do

  • Use only publicly available open-source intelligence
  • Operate within UK law, UK GDPR, and applicable international data standards
  • Provide court-ready, timestamped evidence reports
  • Sign a mutual NDA before every case begins
  • Recommend legal counsel when findings warrant it
  • Refer to Action Fraud, EFCC, NCCC, or police with full documentation

We never do

  • Access private accounts, emails, or messages
  • Intercept communications without a court order
  • Place tracking devices on vehicles without owner consent
  • Conduct physical surveillance or follow individuals
  • Publicly expose or confront any subject directly
  • Work against the interests of vulnerable individuals

About Greyline

Digital-first investigation
for a digital-first world

Greyline Investigations is a professional OSINT and digital intelligence agency, currently registering as a UK company and operating from the United Kingdom. We serve clients across the UK, Nigeria, and the wider diaspora — including the USA, Canada, and Australia. We specialise in investigations conducted entirely online, delivering the same quality of evidence that once required physical surveillance, faster and at a fraction of the cost.

We operate under UK law and in full compliance with the UK GDPR, the Data Protection Act 2018, and the Investigatory Powers Act 2016. For Nigerian client engagements we additionally adhere to the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 and the Cybercrimes (Prohibition, Prevention, Etc.) Act 2015. Every report is prepared to the standard required for court submission in the relevant jurisdiction.

Based in the United Kingdom, with partner investigators covering Nigeria (Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, and Kano). All cases are handled fully remotely — clients communicate with us on UK GMT hours, with flexible scheduling available for US and Canadian clients.
