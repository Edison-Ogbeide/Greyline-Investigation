Digital & Cyber Investigations

Computer Forensics Investigation

Extraction and analysis of digital evidence from computers and devices to uncover data theft, online fraud, unauthorized access, or digital tampering.

Email Tracking and SMS Tracing

Monitoring or tracing the origin of suspicious emails or SMS messages, especially in cyberstalking, fraud, or extortion cases.

Online Social Networking Investigation

Monitoring and analyzing a person's activities, contacts, or suspicious behavior on platforms like Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, or Twitter.

Identity Theft

Investigating the use of someone's personal information (name, ID, bank details) without consent, usually to commit fraud or impersonation.

Fraud & Reputation Investigations

Financial Fraud Investigations

Uncovering deceptive financial schemes such as embezzlement, Ponzi schemes, or fake investment opportunities. Can involve tracing transactions and asset verification.

Real Estate Fraud Investigations

Detecting property scams, fake titles, double sales, or misrepresented real estate deals—common in Nigeria's property market.

Internet Defamation and Slander Investigations

Tracking the source of false, damaging statements made online to harm reputation, often involving legal action or cease-and-desist orders.

Online Defamation Investigation

Focused on digital platforms like blogs, forums, or social media where a person or business has been falsely and maliciously attacked.
