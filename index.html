Confidentiality

Your information is protected

All inquiries are handled under strict confidentiality protocols. Sensitive information is only exchanged after formal agreements are in place. Your identity and case details are never shared with the subject of investigation or any third party.

We operate under UK law, UK GDPR, the Data Protection Act 2018, and the Investigatory Powers Act 2016. For Nigerian engagements, we additionally comply with the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023.

Every report is timestamped, source-documented, and prepared to the standard required for court submission in the relevant jurisdiction.
